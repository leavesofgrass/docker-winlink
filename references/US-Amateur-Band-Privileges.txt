================================================================================
  U.S. AMATEUR RADIO FREQUENCY PRIVILEGES  (quick reference)
================================================================================
Summarized from FCC Rules Part 97 (47 CFR 97.301, 97.303, 97.305) — the
PUBLIC-DOMAIN federal regulation. The full text is in this same folder as
FCC-Part-97.pdf; if anything here disagrees with Part 97, Part 97 governs.

This is the legal allocation. It is NOT the ARRL Band Plan, which additionally
recommends *voluntary* sub-band usage (e.g. where SSB vs. data vs. CW go within
a band). For those conventions see the ARRL Band Plan at
http://www.arrl.org/band-plan  (fetch it when you have Internet).

License classes below: T=Technician, G=General, A=Advanced (grandfathered),
E=Amateur Extra. "P" = phone/image privileges in that segment.
Always confirm current rules — allocations change (e.g. symbol-rate/bandwidth
and new bands). Verified against the 2025 CFR edition.
